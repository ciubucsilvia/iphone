What's new

All models are equipped with the A16 Bionic single-chip system, a 48-megapixel main camera and ProMotion screens with Ceramic Shield protection and a frequency range of 1 - 120 Hz, as well as accelerated LPDDR5 memory. The design of the line has been updated, there are two cutouts on the front panel of the smartphones.

All iPhone 14 in the US will be sold without a SIM card tray: the manufacturer says that the improved eSIM technology will allow you to transfer old electronic SIM cards to new smartphones.

The front camera (it is equipped with autofocus and recognizes the user faster in low light conditions) and the main camera sensor have been updated.

The iPhone 14 Pro came out in a new design - without the signature "bangs". A new color has also appeared in the range. In the smartphone, unlike the "regular" 14th iPhones, the A16 Bionic processor is installed, which can perform 4 trillion photo operations per second.
